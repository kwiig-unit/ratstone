Rat care

Bringing Home New Rats

This video will provide information on rat care for potential new pet rat owners.

Key takeaways

Before bringing your furry friends home ensure you have:

  • Cage set up assembled
  • Watter bottles/food trays
  • Toys, beds, bedding
  • Food
  • Research local small animal vet + hospitals

Day of bringing home

  • Bring a carrier
  • Ensure their room is quiet + dim
  • Let your rats settle in
  • Quarantine them for two weeks

Bonding

  • Be patient!
  • Let them get used to your smell
  • Offer treats
  • Work your way up to petting/handling
