Rat care

A short video on preparing to bring home pet rats

Key takeaways

Before bringing your new furry friends home ensure you have:

  • Cage set up assembled
  • Watter bottles/food trays
  • Toys, beds, bedding
  • Food
  • Research local small animal vet + hospitals

Day of bringing home

  • Bring a carrier
  • Ensure their room is quiet, dim, away from other animals/li>
  • Let your rats settle in
  • Quarantine them for two weeks

Bonding

  • Be patient!
  • Let them get used to your smell
  • Offer treats
  • Work your way up to petting/handling
